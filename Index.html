About Dr. Vignesh Subramani

I am a dedicated nephrologist committed to providing comprehensive and compassionate care for individuals with kidney-related conditions. My journey in medicine has been shaped by training at some of India's most prestigious institutions.

I completed my MBBS and MD in General Medicine from the esteemed Madras Medical College, Chennai. Following this, I pursued my passion for renal sciences, earning a DM in Nephrology from the renowned PGIMER, Chandigarh.

This rigorous training has equipped me with the expertise to manage a wide spectrum of kidney diseases with confidence and care. I am currently practicing at Vijay Superspeciality Hospital, where I continue my mission to improve patient outcomes and quality of life.
